The app provides you with the most efficient route of getting between breweries, cideries, and distilleries around Fort Collins. You can currently select up to nine locations to be included in your route; this includes your starting/ending location. You can choose to travel either by car or bicycle. Brew Route optimizations are based upon time and distance.

You will start by setting where you want to start your route. This will also be your ending location. Once your starting/ending location is set-up you can select the locations you want to include on your route.
