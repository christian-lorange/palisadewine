Alfred Eames Cellars

Paonia

Address: 11931 4050 Rd Paonia
Phone: (970) 527-3269
Alfred Eames Cellars wine is traditionally crafted from grapes grown in the high elevations of the Colorado Rocky Mountains. Our red wines are fermented in open vats, barrel aged in French oak, unfined and unfiltered. Our white wines are barrel fermented and lightly filtered.

Visitors to the winery please call (970-527-3269) for appointment. Wine tastings are only offered at the winery during announced winery events and occasional local festivals. Our wine can be tasted every day from May to October at Delicious Orchards on Highway 133 one mile west of Paonia.

Avant Vineyards

Palisade

Address: 3480 E Rd Palisade
Phone: (970) 216-9908
At Avant Vineyards, the principal wine making philosophy is to keep the process as basic and natural as possible, letting the character of the fruit come through. We make our wine in hand crafted small batches from our own grapes. They are grown along the mighty Colorado River at the base of the Grand Mesa on Colorado’s western slope, where the cool night-breezes coming off the mountains meets the hot sunny days of this high mountain environment. All this makes our select fine wines ridiculously delicious. Because we’re not trying to please everyone. We’re only trying to please you.
