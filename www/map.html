About the HHAp - Fort Collins

We live in an amazing town. As of last time we checked there was somewhere over 600 restaurants and bars in Fort Collins. That doesn't even count the breweries, cideries, and distilleries. All those options can also be a bit overwhelming sometimes. The paradox of choice.

We built this simple app to help us keep track of it all. We found ourselves going to the same couple places over and over. Not because they were superior to everything else (although we do have our favorites - Tacos for Life! ) but because they were the ones at the front of our memories.

HHAP - Fort Collins (the Happy Hour App for Fort Collins) is a list of ~100 restaurants, bars, and breweries in Fort Collins, CO. You can sort by cost, location in town, and ammentities such as outdoor patios and dog friendly establishments. You can also check on happy hour times and offerings around town.
